About Registration


  • GrassRoot is founded on the idea of helping people get involved in their community; for this reason, registration with GrassRoot is fast, simple, and free.
  • Once you submit your information in the form, you will be fully registered as a Member of the GrassRoot Network. Members gain access to a home page from which they may search for events as well as submit events of their own.
  • Because we value our users' privacy, GrassRoot keeps user data strictly confidential.
