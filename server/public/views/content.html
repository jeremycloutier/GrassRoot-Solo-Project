About GrassRoot


  • GrassRoot is a community-driven political event organizer that promotes civic engagement by helping you plan and locate events in your area.
  • To find political events in your area, simply select your state from the drop-down menu on the right, then click "Search"!
  • Join the Network: Register for free as a GrassRoot Member, and promote your own events on the GrassRoot Network!
